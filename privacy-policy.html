Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4/20/2025

1. Introduction

At KawaiMe, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines what we collect (very little), how we use it, and how we prioritize security and simplicity in our app.

2. No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect or store personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or user accounts. You can use KawaiMe anonymously.

3. Image Processing

Uploaded images are used solely for generating your kawaii-style transformation. We do not store images. They are automatically deleted immediately after processing is complete.

4. Payment Handling

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. All payment validation and subscription tracking is handled securely through Apple and RevenueCat. We do not see or store your billing or personal information.

5. Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat – subscription and payment management
  • Firebase (server-side only) – used securely for backend functions (e.g. AI image generation)

6. Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. All communication between the app and server is encrypted. Images are not stored, and no personal data is saved on our servers.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal data, there is no information to request, export, or delete. If you have any questions, we’re happy to help.

8. Future Analytics

In the future, we may use anonymized analytics tools like Firebase Crashlytics and PostHog to help us improve the app and understand usage patterns. These tools help us identify bugs, performance issues, and improve features — never for advertising or profiling.

If and when these tools are added, this policy will be updated, and we will remain transparent about what is collected. No personally identifiable data will be collected without your consent.
